# Product Spec (MVP)

Audience:
- Laymen requiring practical legal procedure guidance.
- Advocates requiring source-heavy, verification-gated legal workflows.

Core boundaries:
- Indian law domain only.
- No final legal advice.
- No unsupported legal assertions.
- Citation verification required for advocate-authoritative outputs.

Critical additions:
- Incident date handling for pre/post 1 July 2024 criminal framework.
- Jurisdiction/state-aware workflow surfaces.
- Source verification dashboard.
- CAPTCHA bypass prohibition.
- Private upload isolation.
- Emergency escalation routing.
- Advocate verification path.
- Audit logging with redacted data.
- Evaluation harness for fail-closed verification.
